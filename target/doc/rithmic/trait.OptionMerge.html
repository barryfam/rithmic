fn merge<F>(self, optb: Self, f: F) -> Self where
    F: FnOnce(Self::T, Self::T) -> Self::T, 

Merges two Options by the following logic:

xyx.merge(y, f)
NoneNoneNone
Some(a)NoneSome(a)
NoneSome(b)Some(b)
Some(a)Some(b)Some(f(a, b))
Examples
use rithmic::OptionMerge;

assert_eq!(Some(3).merge(Some(5), |a, b| a+b), Some(8));
assert_eq!(Some(3).merge(None, |a, b| a+b), Some(3));
assert_eq!(None.merge(Some(5), |a, b| a+b), Some(5));
Notes

If T has an identity element under f(), also consider Option::unwrap_or:

x.unwrap_or(0) + y.unwrap_or(0)
